<p><strong>The C-Band Weather Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Polarization</li><li>Dual Polarization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The C-Band Weather Radar market is categorized into single polarization and dual polarization technologies. Single polarization radar transmits and receives signals in one plane, effectively detecting precipitation and its intensity, making it suitable for basic weather monitoring. In contrast, dual polarization radar uses two orthogonal polarization states, which enhances precipitation type differentiation, improves hydrometeor classification, and provides more detailed information about storm structures. This advancement enables more accurate weather forecasting and better understanding of atmospheric phenomena, catering to diverse meteorological needs.</p></p>
