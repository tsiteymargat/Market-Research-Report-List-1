<p><ul><li>Linear Motor</li><li>Rotary Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Drive Servo Motor market is divided into two primary types: linear motors and rotary motors. Linear motors provide straight-line motion, commonly used in applications requiring precise positioning and high-speed movement, such as in semiconductor manufacturing and packaging. Rotary motors, on the other hand, offer rotational motion and are often utilized in robotics, CNC machinery, and conveyor systems. Both types eliminate the need for belts or gears, resulting in enhanced efficiency, reduced maintenance, and improved accuracy in motion control applications.</p></p>
