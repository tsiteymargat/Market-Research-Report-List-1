<p><p>The military heat exchangers market includes various types designed for specific applications. Shell and tube heat exchangers, known for their robustness, utilize a series of tubes within a shell to transfer heat effectively. Double pipe heat exchangers consist of one pipe nested within another for simple design and easy maintenance. Plate heat exchangers, with their compact structure and high efficiency, feature multiple plates for enhanced heat transfer. Other types may include finned tube, air-cooled, and brazed heat exchangers, catering to diverse military needs.</p></p>
