<p><ul><li>Non-Diastatic</li><li>Diastatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The malt powder market is divided into two main types: non-diastatic and diastatic. Non-diastatic malt powder is produced through kilning and contains no enzymes, primarily used for flavor and color enhancement in baking and brewing. In contrast, diastatic malt powder retains active enzymes, promoting fermentation and sugar conversion, making it ideal for bread making and other fermentative processes. Both types cater to different industry needs, influencing product characteristics and consumer preferences within the food and beverage sector.</p></p>
