<p><ul><li>Walk-in cooler rooms</li><li>Walk-in freezer rooms</li><li>Walk-in hybrid rooms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chilled rooms market encompasses three primary types: walk-in cooler rooms, walk-in freezer rooms, and walk-in hybrid rooms. Walk-in cooler rooms are designed to store perishable goods at controlled temperatures, typically above freezing. Walk-in freezer rooms maintain sub-zero temperatures for long-term storage of frozen products. Walk-in hybrid rooms combine both cooling and freezing capabilities within a single unit, allowing for versatile storage solutions. Together, these types serve various industries, such as food service, pharmaceuticals, and grocery retail, ensuring optimal product preservation.</p></p>
