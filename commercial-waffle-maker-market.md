<p><p>The commercial waffle maker market is categorized into single-grid and double-grid types. In contrast, double-grid waffle makers can prepare two waffles simultaneously, catering to higher demand and increasing efficiency in larger venues. Both types vary in cooking times, sizes, and design features, allowing businesses to choose according to their specific operational needs and customer volume.</p></p>
