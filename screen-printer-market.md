<p><p>The screen printer market is divided into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic screen printers operate with minimal human intervention, streamlining the printing process for high-volume production, efficiency, and precision. They are ideal for large enterprises seeking consistency. On the other hand, semi-automatic screen printers require some manual input, offering flexibility and lower initial costs, making them suitable for small to medium-sized businesses or short-run projects where customization is needed.</p></p>
