<p><ul><li>Stored Pressure Type</li><li>Cartridge-Operated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire extinguisher market is categorized primarily into two types: stored pressure and cartridge-operated. Stored pressure extinguishers have a constant supply of extinguishing agent stored under pressure, allowing for immediate use. They are popular for their simplicity and effectiveness. In contrast, cartridge-operated extinguishers utilize a separate cartridge containing gas that, when activated, propels the extinguishing agent. This type offers advantages in terms of versatility and refillability, making it suitable for various settings and applications.</p></p>
