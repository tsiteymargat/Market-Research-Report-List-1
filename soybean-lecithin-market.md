<p><p>The soybean lecithin market is primarily divided into two types: raw soybean lecithin and refined soybean lecithin. Raw soybean lecithin is derived directly from the crude extraction process and retains higher levels of phospholipids and other natural components, making it suitable for health supplements and animal feed. Refined soybean lecithin undergoes processing to enhance purity and functionality, resulting in a flavorless product ideal for food applications, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. Both types serve diverse industrial needs based on their specific properties.</p></p>
