<p><ul><li>Single-Sided Boards</li><li>Double-Sided Boards</li><li>Multilayer Circuit Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB market comprises three main types: single-sided boards, double-sided boards, and multilayer circuit boards. Single-sided boards feature components on one side, making them cost-effective for simpler applications. Double-sided boards have components on both sides, allowing for more complex circuitry without increasing size. Multilayer circuit boards contain multiple layers of circuitry, enabling intricate designs and higher functionality, suitable for advanced electronic devices. Each type caters to specific industry needs, driving innovation and growth in various technology sectors.</p></p>
