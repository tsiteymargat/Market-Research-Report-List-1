<p><strong>The Occupational Radiation Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Dosimeters</li><li>Active Dosimeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Occupational Radiation Monitoring market comprises two main types: passive dosimeters and active dosimeters. Passive dosimeters, such as thermoluminescent dosimeters (TLDs) and film badges, measure accumulated radiation exposure without requiring power, offering portability and ease of use. In contrast, active dosimeters incorporate electronic components to provide real-time radiation measurements, displaying immediate exposure levels and allowing for timely alerts. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in workplaces where radiation exposure is a concern, such as healthcare and nuclear industries.</p></p>
