<p><p>The Smart Card IC market is primarily divided into two types: memory cards and microprocessor cards. Memory cards function as storage devices, holding data like personal information and transactions, and are often simpler and more cost-effective. Microprocessor cards, on the other hand, contain an embedded microprocessor, enabling advanced processing capabilities, enhanced security, and support for complex applications. These cards can perform computations and encryption, making them suitable for uses in payment systems, identification, and secure access control.</p></p>
