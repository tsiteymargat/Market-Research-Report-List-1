<p><p>The smart electrical meters market is divided into two primary types: single-phase and three-phase meters. Single-phase meters are typically used in residential settings, measuring electricity consumption for homes and small businesses. They provide accurate billing and real-time energy usage data to consumers. Three-phase meters, on the other hand, cater to industrial and commercial applications, managing higher power loads and providing greater efficiency in energy distribution. Both types contribute to enhanced grid management and energy conservation efforts.</p></p>
