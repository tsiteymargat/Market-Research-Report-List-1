<p><strong>The Solar Thermal Power System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parabolic Trough Type</li><li>Power Tower Type</li><li>Dish Type</li><li>Fresnel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar thermal power system market encompasses several key technologies: </p><p>1. **Parabolic Trough Type** uses curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver, heating a fluid that drives a turbine.</p><p>2. **Power Tower Type** employs a central receiver with numerous mirrors reflecting sunlight, producing high temperatures for electricity generation.</p><p>3. **Dish Type** features parabolic dishes that concentrate sunlight onto a receiver, directly powering a Stirling engine.</p><p>4. **Fresnel Type** utilizes flat mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver, providing a compact and efficient solution. Each type offers unique advantages for energy generation.</p></p>
