<p><p>A Fire Alarm Control Panel (FACP) is a critical component in fire detection systems, managing alarms and signals from various devices. The market for FACPs is primarily classified into two types: conventional and addressable. Conventional FACPs operate on zones, where alarms are triggered based on grouped devices, making it simpler but less precise. Addressable FACPs, on the other hand, utilize unique identifiers for each device, allowing for more precise location identification and enhanced monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
