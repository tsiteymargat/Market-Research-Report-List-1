<p><strong>The Tool Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Screw-on Type</li><li>Counter-bore Weld Type</li><li>Flash or Inertia Weld Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tool joint markets are categorized based on the connection methods used for joining drill pipe sections. The threaded screw-on type features screw threads for easy assembly and disassembly, providing reliable strength. Counter-bore weld type involves a welded connection that offers enhanced durability against wear and tear. Flash or inertia weld type utilizes high-pressure welding techniques for a strong bond, minimizing weight and enhancing performance. Each type is tailored for specific applications in drilling operations, balancing strength, maintenance, and efficiency.</p></p>
